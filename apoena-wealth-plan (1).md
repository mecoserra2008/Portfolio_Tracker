# Apoena Wealth - Website Master Plan

## Project Overview

**Company:** Apoena Wealth Management  
**Industry:** Wealth Management & Financial Services  
**Target Audience:** High-net-worth individuals, family offices, institutional investors  
**Brand Heritage:** Indigenous-inspired (Apoena = "one who sees far" in Tupi-Guarani)

## 1. Brand Identity & Design System

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- `#0A4D3C` - Deep Forest Green (Primary)
- `#1A5F4F` - Medium Forest Green (Secondary)
- `#0F0F0F` - Obsidian Black (Text/Headers)
- `#1A1A1A` - Charcoal Black (Backgrounds)

**Accent Colors:**
- `#10B981` - Emerald Green (CTAs)
- `#34D399` - Light Emerald (Hover states)
- `#D4AF37` - Subtle Gold (Premium accents)

**Neutral Colors:**
- `#F8F9FA` - Off-white (Backgrounds)
- `#E5E7EB` - Light Gray (Borders)
- `#6B7280` - Medium Gray (Secondary text)
- `#FFFFFF` - Pure White

### Typography

**Headings:**
- Font Family: 'Inter', 'Montserrat', or 'Playfair Display' for elegance
- Weights: 700 (Bold), 600 (Semi-bold)
- Line Height: 1.2

**Body Text:**
- Font Family: 'Inter' or 'Source Sans Pro'
- Weight: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium)
- Line Height: 1.6
- Size: 16px base

**Special Elements:**
- Numbers/Stats: Tabular numerals, weight 600
- Quotes: Italic, larger size

### Design Principles

1. **Minimalism** - Clean, uncluttered layouts
2. **Sophistication** - Premium feel through spacing and typography
3. **Trust** - Professional imagery, clear hierarchy
4. **Heritage** - Subtle indigenous geometric patterns as background textures
5. **Accessibility** - WCAG 2.1 AA compliance

### Indigenous Design Elements

- Geometric patterns inspired by Tupi-Guarani art (subtle, as textures)
- Natural organic shapes (leaves, water, earth)
- Use in: Section dividers, background patterns (10% opacity), icon designs
- **Note:** Keep subtle - professional first, cultural second

## 5. Content Guidelines

### Voice & Tone

**Professional but Approachable:**
- Use "we" and "you" to create connection
- Avoid excessive jargon
- Explain complex concepts clearly
- Be confident but not arrogant

**Key Messaging Pillars:**
1. **Trust** - Emphasize track record, credentials, transparency
2. **Innovation** - Highlight algorithmic capabilities, technology
3. **Partnership** - Long-term relationships, personalized service
4. **Vision** - Long-term perspective, indigenous wisdom metaphor

### Writing Style

**Do:**
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences concise (< 20 words average)
- Use subheadings for scannability
- Include specific numbers and metrics
- End sections with clear CTAs

**Don't:**
- Make guarantees about returns
- Use hyperbolic language ("best", "guaranteed", "never")
